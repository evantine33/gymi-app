Features

Everything your gym needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Gymi is purpose-built for coaches who want to focus on coaching — not wrestling with software.

Workout Programming
Build daily workouts with exercises, sets, reps, and percentages. Schedule them on your gym's calendar for all members to see.
Member Management
Invite athletes with a simple join code. See who's logging, track engagement, and manage your gym roster from one place.
Volume Tracking
Visualize training volume over time. Help members understand their load and prevent overtraining with data-driven insights.
Benchmarks & PRs
Track personal records and benchmark lifts across your entire gym. Celebrate wins and keep athletes motivated to come back.
Community & Messages
Keep your gym community connected with messaging and a community feed. Build culture, not just fitness.
Gym Store
Sell merchandise, supplements, and programs directly to your members through your gym's built-in store.
